About Us

Welcome to our game!

Here, you can learn more about the game, its rules, and the people behind it. We are a team of passionate developers and designers who love creating fun and engaging experiences for our players.

We hope you enjoy playing our game as much as we enjoyed creating it for you. If you have any questions, suggestions, or feedback, please feel free to get in touch with us.

The aim of the app is to promote sustainability through a fun and educating manner. Located around campus are QR codes and if you scan those, they will take you to a new webpage! You must be logged into an account in order to be able to view these webpages. From there, you can read about the ways in which you can be more sustainable at the exact location you scanned the QR code in, and finally, you can answer a related quiz and earn points! Your points will be displayed on a leaderboard so you can see how you're doing compared to your friends!

Get out and start exploring campus! Goodluck trying to find the QR codes!
