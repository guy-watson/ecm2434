What we are about!

This site is intended to educate University of Exeter students on their effect on our local and global environment. With engaging articles and quizzes, you will be able to put your knowledge to the test to gain points and compete against your friends for who is the most aware of their impact on the world around them!

How do I play?

It's simple! Just sign up and start reading articles and taking quizzes. You will be able to see your progress on the leaderboard and compete against your friends to see who has the highest level of knowledge about sustainability on campus!

How do I earn points?

For every article you read, you will earn 10 points. For every quiz you take, you will earn 20 points. For every quiz you pass, you will earn 50 points.
